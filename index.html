Rushan's beauty

Magnificent Rushan Mountains, Vamar | photo: Artyom Chudakov

Rushon is a village and the seat of Rushon District of Gorno-Badakhshan Autonomous Region in southeastern Tajikistan.

  • 1932 - Formed on October 27 according to the decision of the Bureau of the Gorno-Badakhshan Regional Committee of the Communist Party of Tajikistan
  • 65 km - Rushan is located 65 kilometers north of the capital of the region, the city of Khorog.
  • 6,577 - A total population of jamoat (2015)

Numerous gorges, high mountain peaks, powerful glacial formations create a unique beauty. But for travel, and for local residents, you need to know a short-term weather forecast. The weather in the mountains is prone to sudden changes and unpleasant surprises, in order to avoid unnecessary troubles it is better to prepare for them in advance.

"In 2012, I was lucky to visit Rushan. The beauty of nature, beautiful people, especially the spiritual inner beauty of people have remained in my memory, even though almost 10 years have passed." - Artyom Chudakov
